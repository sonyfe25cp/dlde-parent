Mi-T-M JP-2503-1ME1 - JP Series 2500 PSI Cold Water Electric Pressure Washer
Part of the JP Series collection (View full JP Series collection)
This 3.2 GPM pressure washer is the best in the business. With so many pressure washer models, Mi-T-M makes certain each model is crafted with precision. Electrically driven, this portable pressure washer is easy to use and will last for life. This series holds a direct drive triplex piston pump with ceramic plungers and a totally enclosed fan-cooled electric motor.

Features:

  • 36" two-piece lance with rubber grip
  • Thermal and safety relief valves
  • In-line water strainer
  • 3/16" powder coated steel base plate
  • Stainless steel and brass unloader with adjustable pressure valve and forged brass manifold
  • 35' cord with ground fault circuit interrupter (GFCI)

Specifications:

  • Max PSI: 2500
  • Engine: 6.0 HP electric
  • Voltage: 230 volts
  • Water flow: 3.2 GPM
  • Water temperature: cold water
  • Hose: 25' (3/8")
  • Warranty: 1 year limited parts and 7 year limited pump warranty
  • Dimensions: 44" L x 26" W x 27" H
