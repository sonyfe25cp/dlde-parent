Woodland Factory Showroom
1460 Churchill Downs Avenue, Woodland, CA  95776 

SHOWROOM HOURS:  Monday - Friday 8:00am - 5:00pm,  Saturday 8:30am - 4:30pm,  Closed Sundays

1-800-242-1442   or   1-530-666-1442

FROM SACRAMENTO:  Take Interstate 5 North towards Woodland. Once you get to Woodland, take the HWY 113 North Exit [EXIT# 538] (Yuba City / East Street Exit). At the bottom of the off-ramp (stop-light) make a right turn onto HWY 113 North. Take your next immediate right (very next street) onto Churchill Downs Avenue. We are located about 1/2 mile down at 1460 Churchill Downs Avenue.
 

FROM REDDING:  Take Interstate 5 South towards Sacramento.  Once you get to Woodland take the HWY 113 North [EXIT# 538] (Yuba City / East Street Exit).
At the bottom of the off-ramp (stop-light) make a right turn onto HWY 113 North. Go under the freeway and make a right turn at the next street -- Churchill Downs Avenue. We are located about 1/2 mile down at 1460 Churchill Downs Avenue.
 

FROM SAN FRANCISCO:  Take Interstate 80 East towards Sacramento. After you pass the city of Dixon and right before you get to the city of Davis, take the HWY 113 North exit towards Woodland.  Stay on HWY 113 North towards Woodland.  As you get into the Woodland area, HWY 113 N. will merge right on to Interstate 5 North. As HWY 113 N. merges into Interstate 5 - stay in the Right lane and get off the freeway onto the HWY 113 North Exit [EXIT# 538] (Yuba City / East Street Exit). At the bottom of the off-ramp (stop-light) make a right turn onto HWY 113 N.  Take your next immediate right (very next street) onto Churchill Downs Avenue. We are located about 1/2 mile down at 1460 Churchill Downs Avenue.
